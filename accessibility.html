Our commitment

GrubMarket is committed to making its websites, marketplace, and software accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We want every customer to be able to source, sell, and order food with ease.

Standards we follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and to align with applicable requirements such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

What we do

  • Support keyboard navigation and visible focus across interactive elements.
  • Use semantic markup and ARIA labels so assistive technologies can interpret content.
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast and scalable, legible typography.
  • Provide text alternatives for meaningful images.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We test regularly, train our teams, and work to remediate issues as we identify them.

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier or need content in an alternative format, please tell us through our contact page and we'll work to help.
