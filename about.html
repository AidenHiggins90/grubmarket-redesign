Who we are

A food technology company on a mission to digitally transform the American food supply chain.

Founded in 2014, GrubMarket is the largest private food technology company in the U.S. We operate across the U.S. and Canadian food supply chain — serving business customers and end consumers, and providing software-as-a-service that digitally transforms businesses at every step from farm to table.

What we do

We connect growers, distributors, businesses, and homes through three tightly integrated offerings: a B2B marketplace for buying and selling food, an AI-powered software platform (ERP, eCommerce, payments, and enterprise AI), and farm-fresh delivery to homes and offices through local food hubs.

Where we operate

GrubMarket operates in Arizona, California, Connecticut, Georgia, Michigan, New York, New Jersey, Missouri, Massachusetts, Nevada, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Texas, Washington, and British Columbia (Canada), with plans to expand across the rest of the U.S., Canada, and beyond.
