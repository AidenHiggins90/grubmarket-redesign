Who we are

A food technology company on a mission to digitally transform the American food supply chain.

Founded in 2014, GrubMarket is the largest private food technology company in the U.S. We operate across the U.S. and Canadian food supply chain — serving business customers and end consumers, and providing software-as-a-service that digitally transforms businesses at every step from farm to table. Today the company does business across all 50 U.S. states and in nine countries internationally, with commerce touching more than 70 countries worldwide.

What we do

We connect growers, distributors, businesses, and homes through three tightly integrated offerings. Our B2B marketplace lets grocers, restaurants, schools, and government buyers source thousands of products at exclusive wholesale prices. Our AI-powered software platform — anchored by WholesaleWare, the food industry's fastest-growing cloud ERP — runs inventory, warehouse operations, grower accounting, and general ledger for wholesalers and distributors, while GrubAssist (the industry's first virtual AI assistant), AI Orders, Orders IO, and GrubPay automate ordering, payments, and business insight. And our farm-fresh delivery network, through local hubs like Good Eggs and FreshGOGO, brings groceries to homes and offices for up to 50% less than retail.

How we grow

GrubMarket has grown to more than 70 acquisitions by combining strategic buys with organic expansion of its software and marketplace. Recent additions include Procurant, a SaaS trading platform facilitating $5.5B in annual GMV for 850+ customers across 14 countries; Coast Citrus Distributors, the largest acquisition in company history and a major U.S. supplier of tropical produce; and Schoenmann Produce, a Houston-based distributor serving the Gulf Coast for over a century. In February 2026, GrubMarket raised a $50 million Series H round at an approximately $4.5 billion pre-money valuation, led by Future Food Fund and Portfolia Funds, to fuel further AI and eCommerce expansion.

Recognition

GrubMarket has been named to the CNBC Disruptor 50 list for three consecutive years (2023–2025), recognized on the inaugural TIME100 Companies Industry Leaders list, and honored with The Shelby Report's Sustainability in the Food Industry award for its Sustainable California initiative — which has sponsored the planting of more than 230,000 trees and funded organic certification for underserved farmers through partnerships with One Tree Planted and CCOF.

Where we operate

GrubMarket operates in Arizona, California, Connecticut, Georgia, Michigan, New York, New Jersey, Missouri, Massachusetts, Nevada, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Texas, Washington, and British Columbia (Canada), with plans to expand across the rest of the U.S., Canada, and beyond.
