Community, not just customer support

GrubMarket's origins as a network of local CSA farms and food hubs — going back to the Farmigo platform, which connected thousands of small farms to more than 3,000 buyer communities — mean the company has always depended on real relationships between growers, distributors, and the people who buy from them, not just software. The Community Hub is a continuation of that: a place to trade practical advice with other operators who've solved the same warehouse or pricing problem, not a support ticket queue.
