GrubMarket acquires Schoenmann Produce, a century-old Gulf Coast distributor

The acquisition of a respected Houston-based distributor — serving the Gulf Coast for over 100 years — strengthens GrubMarket's presence across Texas and the broader region.

Acquisition · April 15, 2026

We are thrilled to announce that GrubMarket has completed the acquisition of Schoenmann Produce, a highly respected Houston-based distributor that has been serving the Gulf Coast region for over 100 years.

This partnership strengthens our presence in Texas and the broader Gulf Coast region as we continue to drive forward our mission to modernize the food supply chain through technology and innovation.

Over a century of excellence

Schoenmann Produce has built a strong reputation for quality, service, and reliability, supported by impressive infrastructure and long-standing customer and partner relationships established since the company's founding in 1910. Its capabilities include:

  • A state-of-the-art 90,000 sq ft facility on 34.5 acres near Houston's Hobby Airport
  • 75,000 sq ft of cold storage, a fully refrigerated dock, and advanced ripening rooms
  • A Primus score of 100 — best-in-class food safety, traceability, and quality control
  • Deep relationships across foodservice, retail, wholesale, healthcare, education, and maritime sectors
  • A strong commitment to Texas-grown produce and local agriculture
Joining GrubMarket reflects an important moment in the continued evolution of Schoenmann Produce.Mark Steakley, President, Schoenmann Produce
Schoenmann Produce has built an impressive business with real staying power in one of the country's most important regional produce markets.Mike Xu, CEO of GrubMarket

Future growth powered by innovation

As part of GrubMarket, the company will gain access to proprietary, AI-powered software tools including WholesaleWare, GrubAssist AI, AI Orders, Orders IO, and GrubPay — reinforcing GrubMarket's mission to modernize and strengthen the food supply chain through strategic growth and cutting-edge technology.
