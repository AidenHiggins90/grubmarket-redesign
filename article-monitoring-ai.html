GrubMarket announces Monitoring AI Agent to detect and alert on critical business issues

A new GrubAssist AI agent that continuously monitors key business data, detects anomalies, and sends proactive alerts — shifting teams from reactive firefighting to proactive control.

Enterprise AI · January 28, 2026

Food supply chain operators face pressure to move quickly, remain efficient, and navigate constant change while managing perishability, complex logistics, and shifting customer demand. GrubMarket has released a new specialized AI agent — the Monitoring AI Agent — within GrubAssist AI.

It provides continuous, passive monitoring of key business and operational data, automatically detecting anomalies, identifying market condition changes, and surfacing shifts in critical performance metrics. It sends proactive alerts based on custom trigger conditions, enabling teams to identify issues sooner and respond more rapidly.

From reactive firefighting to proactive control

Many food businesses operate reactively: problems surface after they occur, reporting happens manually, and time is scarce. Although data exists across ERP, eCommerce, payments, and finance systems, most teams lack the bandwidth for continuous analysis. The Monitoring AI Agent shifts businesses from reactive to proactive operations, with configurable alerts for scenarios such as:

  • A key customer has gone more than 7 days without placing an order
  • A vendor shortage is projected to impact outgoing sales orders
  • A new customer has exceeded its credit limit

Five core capabilities

  • Proactive monitoring. Passively monitors critical variables, detecting anomalies before they impact results.
  • Custom monitoring rules. Configure metrics across inventory, margins, customer activity, vendor performance, and AR aging.
  • Always-on alerts. Surfaces exceptions with proactive email notifications to designated team members.
  • Faster decision-making. Alerts include supporting context for quick corrective action.
  • Seamless integration. Works across ERP and accounting systems including WholesaleWare ERP, Famous Software, Produce Pro, Thyme Software, and QuickBooks.
