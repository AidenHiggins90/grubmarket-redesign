A growing family of AI agents

Enterprise AI isn't one tool — it's a fast-expanding roster of specialized agents inside GrubAssist, each built for a specific job. The Sales AI Agent discovers prospects by territory, reads a restaurant's menu or order guide, and generates a customer-ready price sheet in minutes instead of hours. The Monitoring AI Agent watches inventory, margins, customer activity, and receivables around the clock and emails your team the moment something needs attention — a customer who's gone quiet, a vendor shortage, a credit limit exceeded. The Reporting AI Agent turns raw operational data into automated, proactive business insight without anyone running a report by hand.

Every agent plugs into the ERP you already run — WholesaleWare, Famous Software, Produce Pro, Thyme Software, QuickBooks, and more — so none of this requires ripping out your existing systems.
