What you'll actually see on the floor

Rather than a generic product pitch, GrubMarket's booth teams typically run live demos against real scenarios: an AI Orders agent parsing an actual customer email into a structured order in real time, or a Sales AI Agent building a price sheet from a photographed menu on the spot. It's a deliberate choice — distributors have seen enough vaporware demos to be skeptical, so the team would rather show software working against messy, real-world inputs than a polished slide.
