Why we show up in person

GrubMarket's software serves an industry that still runs largely on relationships built face to face — produce buyers who've worked with the same growers for decades, distributors who trust a handshake more than a sales deck. Trade shows and investor events are where that trust gets built: a chance to demo AI Orders on an actual order guide, walk through WholesaleWare with a warehouse manager who's skeptical of "another system," or explain to investors what a $50 million Series H at a $4.5 billion valuation actually funds.
