GrubMarket announces the Spring 2026 release of its AI-powered software ecosystem

Significant new capabilities across WholesaleWare, Orders IO, and AI Orders — the industry's only fully integrated platform spanning ERP, AI, and eCommerce.

Software · May 12, 2026

GrubMarket has announced the Spring 2026 product release for its software suite, introducing significant enhancements across WholesaleWare, Orders IO, and AI Orders.

As the industry's only fully integrated technology platform spanning ERP, AI, and eCommerce, the Spring 2026 Release delivers new capabilities designed to help restaurant and foodservice distributors and produce wholesalers improve efficiency, elevate customer service, and drive profitability.

WholesaleWare enhancements

  • Automated work orders. Streamlines repacking and processing through automated creation and management of work orders, with real-time inventory and cost updates.
  • New price groups. Flexible, rule-based pricing segmentation with item- and category-level overrides.
  • Automated FIFO lot allocation. Optionally allocate lots automatically at shipment, in addition to pre-allocating and scanning to pick.
  • Expanded EDI integrations. New integrations with Produce Alliance and Restaurant365 for seamless, automated order processing.
  • New reports and platform enhancements. Purchasing guides, route summaries, charge insights, and improvements across sales orders, inventory, lot tracking, and accounting.

AI Orders enhancements

  • Multi-language order processing. Supports multi-language inbound orders for diverse customer bases.
  • Match confidence indicators. Visual cues show the AI's confidence in product and unit-of-measure matches.
  • Customer product code recognition. Understands customer-specific product codes, enabling EDI workflows without integration.
  • Customer-specific AI prompts. Tailored AI training for individual customers and their order formats.
  • Enterprise dictionary. Helps AI Orders interpret specialized terminology used by companies and their customers.

Orders IO enhancements

  • Automatic product suggestions. Dynamic, customer-specific upsell recommendations to increase order value.
  • Advanced delivery management. Granular delivery scheduling with product- and customer-specific rules, plus holiday and non-delivery day handling.
  • Order guide tagging. Customizable tags, including for seasonal menus.
  • Streamlined customer onboarding. Automated welcome communications with access to the web and mobile ordering apps.
These innovations reflect our commitment to equipping customers with powerful, AI-driven tools.Mike Xu, CEO of GrubMarket

Continuing innovation across the food supply chain

As foodservice distributors and produce wholesalers face increasing operational complexity and intensifying competition, GrubMarket continues to advance its software platforms to meet the evolving needs of the industry — reducing manual effort, improving operational accuracy, and supporting greater profitability.
