GrubMarket named to the TIME100 Companies Industry Leaders list

TIME recognizes GrubMarket among the companies making an extraordinary impact across their industries — reflecting our progress transforming the food supply chain through AI, eCommerce, and vertical SaaS.

Company · April 29, 2026

We are pleased to share that GrubMarket has been named to the inaugural TIME100 Companies Industry Leaders list, recognizing companies making an extraordinary impact across their respective industries.

This recognition reflects our continued progress in transforming the food supply chain through AI, eCommerce, and vertical SaaS solutions, as well as the strong momentum we've built over the past year.

Recognition by TIME

According to TIME's 2026 Most Influential Companies coverage, GrubMarket has established itself as both a leading technology provider and a major operator within the food supply chain. The company has scaled rapidly by combining strategic acquisitions with its AI-powered WholesaleWare platform, enabling food wholesalers and distributors to modernize their operations through automation, data-driven decision making, and digital infrastructure.

TIME evaluated companies based on impact, innovation, ambition, and success, highlighting GrubMarket's unique position at the intersection of technology and the global food ecosystem.

A year of growth and innovation

This recognition follows a year of significant expansion and technological advancement. Key milestones include accelerated development of agentic AI solutions designed to automate complex workflows and improve operational efficiency — including the Inventory Management AI Agent, Reporting AI Agent, and Monitoring AI Agent.

GrubMarket also continued its strategic growth through acquisitions: Delta Fresh Produce (April 2025), expanding presence in Arizona; Coast Citrus (June 2025), the largest acquisition to date and a major U.S. supplier of tropical produce; and Procurant (November 2025), adding over 850 software customers with a leading SaaS trading platform.

Commitment to sustainability

Sustainability remains a core pillar of GrubMarket's long-term strategy. Through the Sustainable California initiative, the company has sponsored the planting of over 230,000 trees in at-risk regions across the state, and continues to support underserved farmers in achieving organic certification through grants, mentorship, and technical assistance.

We are honored to be recognized by TIME as one of the TIME100 Companies Industry Leaders. This recognition reflects our team's relentless pursuit of operational excellence and sustainable growth, as well as our progress against our mission to transform the food supply chain through AI, eCommerce, vertical Software-as-a-Service, and other technological innovations.Mike Xu, Founder & CEO of GrubMarket

Looking ahead

GrubMarket will continue to expand its presence across the United States and internationally, while advancing the AI-driven transformation of the food supply chain — building and deploying solutions that automate manual processes, improve operational performance, and drive profitability, while continuing to invest in sustainability initiatives that support a healthier, more resilient food system.
