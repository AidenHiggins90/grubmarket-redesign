Where the catalog comes from

GrubMarket's catalog isn't a warehouse of dropshipped goods — it's an aggregation of GrubMarket's own network of growers and distributors, from long-standing regional players like Coast Citrus Distributors, a century-old Gulf Coast supplier, to family growers selling their first harvest through the platform. Buying through the catalog means one account and one delivery schedule instead of juggling a dozen supplier relationships.

Pricing is wholesale by default and improves with volume, and every order routes through the same fulfillment network used for GrubMarket's home delivery brands — so the freshness standards are consistent whether you're a 40-seat restaurant or a regional grocery chain.
