GrubMarket announces Sales AI Agent to transform prospecting and quote creation

A new AI agent helps wholesale food distribution sales teams find prospects, analyze menus, build price sheets, and send proposals — in a single workflow.

Enterprise AI · June 8, 2026

GrubMarket has announced the launch of Sales AI Agent, the newest specialized AI solution within the GrubMarket AI platform.

Designed specifically for wholesale food distribution sales teams, Sales AI Agent helps users identify prospective customers, analyze menus and order guides, generate professional price sheets, and deliver proposals through a single AI-powered workflow.

By combining prospect discovery, menu analysis, automated quote creation, and proposal delivery, Sales AI Agent helps distributors scale prospecting efforts, respond faster to opportunities, and compete more effectively in today's increasingly competitive market.

Key capabilities

  • Territory-based prospect discovery. Search by ZIP code or explore an interactive map to identify restaurants, foodservice operators, and retailers within target markets, with detailed business profiles to build targeted campaigns.
  • AI-powered menu analysis. Upload photos or PDFs of menus and order guides, and the agent intelligently identifies products and recommends matching catalog items.
  • Automated price sheet generation. Create professional, customer-ready price sheets using configured price groups or custom pricing — cutting the time to prepare quotes.
  • Flexible quote customization. Review, edit, add, or remove products and pricing to tailor each proposal to a customer's specific needs.
  • Multi-channel sharing. Share price sheets via email, text, WhatsApp, and more, or download and print them.
  • Seamless integration. Works with WholesaleWare ERP, Thyme Software, Granite State Software, PICS by WaudWare, and Orders IO — and as a standalone offering.

Streamlining the sales process

Traditionally, researching prospects, reviewing menus, selecting products, and building customized quotes can take sales representatives hours for a single opportunity. Sales AI Agent compresses this process into minutes.

Users can define a target geographic area, discover prospective customers, upload menus or order guides, automatically map products to their catalog, apply pricing, and generate polished, customer-ready proposals — all within a single workflow. By automating these manual tasks, distributors can engage more prospects, shorten sales cycles, and improve overall sales productivity.
