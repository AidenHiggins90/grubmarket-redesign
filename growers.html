Built on tools made for CSAs and small farms

Much of GrubMarket's grower-facing technology traces back to Farmigo, a platform trusted by thousands of CSA farms across 25 states, which has helped connect farms to more than 3,000 communities and moved over 30 million pounds of produce directly from growers to members since 2009. That same infrastructure — share management, recurring billing, pickup and delivery logistics — now sits behind GrubMarket's grower accounting and wholesale onboarding, so whether you sell direct-to-consumer through a CSA or wholesale to grocers and restaurants, you're on software built specifically for how a farm actually operates, not a retail system retrofitted for produce.
