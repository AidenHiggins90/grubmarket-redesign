A team built by acquisition and by design

Genevieve Wang's team shipped the Spring 2026 software release across WholesaleWare, Orders IO, and AI Orders, and followed it with the Sales AI Agent — a pattern that repeats across GrubMarket's leadership: ship a capability, then use it internally before rolling it out network-wide. Much of the executive bench arrived the same way GrubMarket's products did — through acquisition, with founders and operators from companies like Coast Citrus Distributors and Schoenmann Produce staying on to run the businesses they built, now with GrubMarket's software and balance sheet behind them.
