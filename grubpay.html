Why payments live inside the platform

Most distributors collect payment through a system that doesn't talk to the one that generated the invoice — so someone spends hours a week matching bank deposits back to open orders by hand. Because GrubPay is embedded in WholesaleWare and Orders IO rather than bolted on, a payment automatically closes the invoice it belongs to the moment it clears, whether a customer paid through the mobile app, over the phone, or by ACH.

That single change — payments and orders sharing one data layer — is what shortens the gap between delivery and cash, and it's the same principle behind everything else in the GrubMarket platform: one system, not five stitched together.
