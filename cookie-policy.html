1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites function, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential — required for the site and marketplace to work, including sign-in and checkout.
  • Preferences — remember choices such as your region or language.
  • Analytics — help us understand traffic and improve the experience.
  • Marketing — measure and tailor the relevance of our communications.

3. Managing your choices

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through our consent banner, and control cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the site work.

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use for analytics and payments. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

6. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies? Reach out through our contact page. See also our Privacy Policy.
