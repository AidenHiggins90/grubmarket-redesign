Real partners, real transitions

When Isabel Freeland, President & CEO of Coast Citrus Distributors, joined GrubMarket in its largest acquisition to date, she pointed to the company's "ownership mentality" culture and GrubMarket's investment in AI as reasons the fit made sense. Mark Steakley of Schoenmann Produce, a Houston distributor with over a century of history, described joining GrubMarket as "an important moment in the continued evolution" of a business his family had run for generations. Neither story is about being absorbed — both kept their name, their team, and their customers, and added a software and AI layer neither had the resources to build alone.
